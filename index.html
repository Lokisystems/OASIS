About Us

Our Story of Hope and Transformation

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Oasis of Hope Initiatives emerged from a vision to create a comprehensive healthcare and empowerment hub in Bulapesa, Isiolo County. What started as a small community health initiative has grown into a vital center serving thousands of families.

We believe that true community development comes from addressing both physical health needs and economic empowerment, creating a sustainable foundation for future generations.

Our Mission

To provide accessible healthcare services and empower communities through sustainable development programs, fostering hope and transformation in Bulapesa and surrounding areas.

Our Vision

A healthy, empowered, and self-sustaining community where every individual has access to quality healthcare and opportunities for growth.
